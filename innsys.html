⚡ Intelligent Inventory & Network System

Real-time stock visibility, automated reordering, and cloud-based access for shipping & warehouse operations. Built during my internship for Expoceylon's Export & Transport department.

100% Solo Development

📋 Overview

INNSYS (Intelligent Inventory & Network System) is a unified platform built specifically for Expoceylon's Export & Transport department. It digitizes warehouse and shipping operations with live dashboards, health monitoring, and automated workflows.


Stock Health: 71.8% Healthy · 12.8% Low Stock · 15.4% Out of Stock


Key Stats: 39 Total Items · 5 Low Stock · 6 Out of Stock · Rs. 1,119,032.59 Stock Value

👨‍💻 My Role (Solo Developer)

  • Full Stack Developer - Entire system built by me
  • QR Code Generator & Scanner Integration
  • Complete Database Architecture & Design
  • Real-time Attendance Validation Logic
  • Report Generation Module (PDF/Excel)
  • UI/UX Design & Frontend Development
  • Server Deployment & Configuration

⚙️ Key Features

  • Item Master – manage all inventory items
  • Stock In / Stock Out – real-time transaction logging
  • Supplier Management – vendor tracking & purchase orders
  • Approval Flow – multi-level validation
  • Automated Reordering – low stock alerts (5 items need reorder)
  • Cloud-based Access – secure remote monitoring
  • Real-time Stock Tracking – live dashboard with health overview

📊 Live Dashboard Features: Total Items (39), Low Stock (5), Out of Stock (6), Stock Value (Rs. 1,119,032.59). Stock Health Overview: 71.8% Healthy, 12.8% Low Stock, 15.4% Out of Stock. Automated reordering for items below reorder level.

✨ Note: This entire system was conceptualized, designed, developed, tested, and deployed by me during my IT Internship at Expoceylon PLC. No external development team involved — 100% self-built solution now actively used by the Export & Transport department.
